How it Works
1. Upload list of Interests
Upload the list of topics and field of studies you'd like to track news for. You can add them in a bulk by uploading a csv or add individually.
2. AI powered algorithms scan news sources
Our AI searches for news mentions of these prospects in more than 400,000+ sources.
3. Get notified with news articles
You get news results featuring the topics in which you are interested in your feed and also a mail based on the frequency set by you.
