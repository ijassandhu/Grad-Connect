Post updates
You can Post updates, polls, attachments, pictures, media on the platform and can also filter your audience based on requirements. Further you can Follow Alums, Students, Faculty members, Bots, Clubs & Committees you are interested in get updates from them in the frequency you like.
Refresh memories
Find pictures of events that happened in the past on a timeline format. Quickly browse through the years you were in the campus and re-live your nostalgic moments with your friends.
Job referrals
AlmaConnect automatically fetches job openings in companies where your alums work. You can further find openings you are interested in and can get referred by the alum(s) working there.
Manage events
Say bye-bye to google forms & all your ad hoc mechanisms to organize an event. Your one stop solution for alumni events - Create an event, setup custom registration form, collect payments, send invitations & reminders, interact with people who have registered, further post updates of the event and much more.
Discover people nearby
See on world map where alums are living and discover the ones living near you. Nothing better to spend time with like-minded folks in your vicinity.
Member directory
AlmaConnect uses Big Data techniques to bring you the most accurate information of alums fetched from various sources and helps you always stay updated with whereabouts of your alums. It also helps you sync your profile with your Facebook / Linkedin account and keep your profile auto-updated.
Admin features
Take control of the network - manage registrations, invitations, content posted on the network. Get access to logs, announcements, master data, mentoring tool & industry connect tool. Sync your official social media accounts too and consolidate your currently fragmented communications with alumni. And much more.
